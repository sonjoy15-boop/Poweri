Premium Service Packages

1. Power Supply Connectivity for Industry/Plant

A single subscription end-to-end power connection service covering online & offline application processing, technical and regulatory approvals, fee and deposit management, infrastructure setup, metering management, CEIG inspection to approvals, and final load release—ensuring seamless transition from application to energized supply.

2. Electrical Inspector Approvals Post-Commissioning

Comprehensive CEIG Compliance Package including drawing approvals, safety clearance documentation, site inspection coordination, post-commissioning liaison with the Electrical Inspector, and ongoing support for periodic inspections to ensure full regulatory compliance.

3. Solar Plant Net Metering Approvals

Provide an end-to-end net metering service for a rooftop or ground-mounted solar plant that takes the customer from initial application through to energized supply and handover. The service covers online and offline application preparation and submission, technical and regulatory approvals, payments and deposits, infrastructure and metering installation, inspections, and final load release to ensure a seamless transition from proposal to commissioned operation.
